Artists Are Pulling Their Music From Spotify — And They’re Not Quiet About It

By Rod  |  Published: September 1, 2025

Streaming has always been a love–hate relationship for musicians, but this summer it turned into open rebellion. A growing number of artists are removing their music from Spotify in protest, calling out the company’s ties to military AI and its CEO’s controversial investments.

The message is clear: streaming shouldn’t come at the cost of ethics.
